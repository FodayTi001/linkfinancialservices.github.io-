GIBRIL IBRAHIM KAMARA ESQ

Chief Executive Officer

Founder and Chief Executive Officer — Link Financial Services (SL) Limited.

BSc, MSc, LLB, BL in Accounting & Finance; Diploma in Project Management; awaiting ACCA. Barrister & Solicitor of High Court of Sierra Leone.

A visionary leader with expertise in financial & management accounting, auditing, risk & performance management and taxation.


  • Financial & management accounting
  • Auditing & risk management
  • Business strategy & performance

Profile
A visionary and results-oriented leader, Gibril Ibrahim Kamara brings a wealth of expertise in financial and management accounting, business strategy, auditing and risk management, performance management, and taxation to his role as Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Link Financial Services Sierra Leone Limited. Driven by his extensive experience in finance, coupled with his deep understanding of the local landscape, he is committed to building a platform that fosters true financial inclusion. This means more than just access; it means empowering individuals and businesses with the knowledge, tools, and opportunities to manage their finances effectively, build wealth, and participate fully in the digital economy. With considerable experience cultivated over the years in professional services, Gibril possesses a keen eye for detail, unwavering diligence, and a deep understanding of the importance of time management and meeting critical deadlines. His collaborative spirit and flexibility make him an invaluable team player, consistently demonstrating a proactive approach and a passion for achieving excellence. He thrives on challenges and is driven by a continuous desire to learn and adapt within dynamic organizational environments. Prior to founding Link Financial Services Sierra Leone Limited, Gibril founded and served as the Chief Executive Officer at GIS Construction Estates and Logistics Co Ltd from May 2014 till date. During this period, he has gained tremendous experience in finance, logistics, and transportation across diverse organizational structures, consistently earning excellent performance ratings. His professional journey includes significant roles such as Finance Officer at CART/SL (May 2012 to 2014) and Banking Officer – Credit Administration (SME Unit) at Union Trust Bank Ltd (February 2009 to May 2012), demonstrating a strong foundation in financial operations and risk assessment. Earlier in his career, he also served as Transport Officer at Safecon (SL) Ltd (October 2008 to January 2009). Beyond his executive roles, Gibril is a dedicated entrepreneur and community leader. He is the Founder and Chairman of the Foundation for Gender and Development Initiative Sierra Leone (FGDI/SL), showcasing his initiative and commitment to making a positive impact.
